Hong Kong MA BRDR Search Project

An AI-powered search experience over Hong Kong Monetary Authority (HKMA) regulatory documents, built to help staff find Basel and banking supervision guidance quickly and accurately.

Scope and Coverage

  • Corpus size: approximately 1,500 documents
  • Source: https://brdr.hkma.gov.hk/eng/main
  • Timeframe: documents available up to July 2025
  • Focus areas:
    • Basel framework (Basel III, “final reforms”), capital adequacy, and disclosure
    • Liquidity requirements (e.g., LCR/NSFR), stress testing, and supervisory policy
    • Pillars 1–3 guidance, reporting templates, FAQs, circulars, and related HKMA materials

What You Can Do

  • Search HKMA regulatory texts in natural language and get targeted results
  • Jump to key passages, definitions, and tables across circulars and guidance
  • Discover related documents and refine queries quickly

Example Questions

  • When Basel III final reform was implemented?
  • What are Basel III requirements for Tier 1 banks?
  • How does HKMA define Capital Adequacy Ratio (CAR) and disclosure obligations?
  • What are the reporting timelines for Pillar 3 disclosures?
  • What liquidity metrics does HKMA require and how are they calculated?

How It Works

This site uses the Google Cloud Gen App Builder search widget to index and query the HKMA BRDR corpus. Click the “Search here” field to open the assistant, enter your question, and browse the most relevant documents.

For authoritative guidance, always refer to the source documents on the HKMA BRDR website.

Last updated: July 2025 snapshot. Source: HKMA BRDR (Hong Kong).
